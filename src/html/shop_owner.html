Types of plastics

Plastics can be classified into two types:

  1. Thermosets
  2. Thermoplastics

Thermosets are materials that are molded and then cured into their final forms and include materials like polyurethane or silicone. Typically, these are difficult to recycle by the means of mechanical recycling.

Thermoplastics become pliable or workable after being heated past their melting temperatures, and most types of thermoplastic can be melted down and recycled.

Examples include:

  • PET
  • PP
  • HDPE
  • PLA
  • ABS
  • PC
  • Nylon

What machines would you need

Plastic Shredder

  shreds plastic parts into particles

Plastic Extruder

  melts plastic particles and extrudes through a profile

Spool Winder

  winds extruded plastics onto a spool

Filament Palletizer

  cuts filament into small pallet (for injection-molding)

Filament Pultruder

  recycle PET bottles into usable 3D filament
